  <h1>Discover Zurich</h1>
  <p>
    Start things off by wandering through Zurich’s old town and marvelling at its historic buildings, then make a stop at the Lindenhof and enjoy the view of the city and the lake: and this is just the start of your tour of discovery through Zurich.
  </p>
  <h2>Packages</h2>
  <p>
   With the Zürich Card, you benefit from many discounts in restaurants, museums and shops and travel free of charge on public transport. An additional free luxury: Zurich has 1,200 fountains that provide fresh drinking water.
  </p>
